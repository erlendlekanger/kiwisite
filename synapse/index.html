//WHAT IS SYNAPSE_

SYNAPSE_ is a mind whose brain is for sale by the piece. It is split into – plots built from – cells; each plot can be held by exactly one Solana wallet, and the whole brain, every cell of it, costs exactly 1,000 SOL. Nothing is ever added to the map. What you see is the entire supply.

The mind stays awake while it is being bought. Every day it falls asleep, dreams, and hides bounties inside the dream. They are hard to earn. Whoever cracks one is paid from the mind's own wallet, together with a few secretly chosen landowners whose planted thoughts built that night's dream. The rest of this page explains the map, the money, the dreams, and the terms you accept by using it.

//THE BRAIN

The map is a side view of the brain in six regions: instinct, perception, memory, logic, creative and social. Regions are split into plots of wildly different sizes, generated once from a fixed seed and never reshaped. A plot's cell count is the only variable that matters: it sets the price, and it sets your weight when the mind decides whose thoughts to dream from.

A plot is always in one of three visible states: lit means unclaimed, dark means taken, and a picture means taken by someone who decorated it. Owners upload any image and it is clipped into the plot's shape for everyone to see. Plots held by the wallet you are signed in with carry a small light running along their border.

//BUYING & SELLING

Buying is simple: connect a Phantom wallet, click an unclaimed plot, and buy it for cells × – SOL, paid as native SOL on mainnet to the mind's treasury:

–

Starting a purchase reserves the plot to you for a short window so nobody can snipe it mid-payment. The server reads the transaction on-chain and grants the plot only once the transfer is confirmed; if two wallets race, exactly one wins and the other is rejected before paying. The chain is final. There are no refunds, ever.

Money flows back out two ways: resales and bounties. Resale: list a plot at any SOL price, a buyer pays your wallet directly, the server verifies the transfer on-chain, and the plot moves. The treasury takes nothing on resales. Bounties: see the dreams below.

The SYNAPSE_ WALLET counter at the top of the brain is the treasury's real on-chain balance, valued in USD at the live SOL price. It grows from land sales and creator fees, and it is the pool every bounty is paid from.

//THE DREAMS

Every dream-day the mind dreams a new world, reachable from ENTER THE DREAM. Six tasks wait inside, one per region, tiered from EASY to EXTREME, each carrying a bounty. The board tells you only that something waits, and roughly where. Nothing anywhere explains what a task is or how to do it. That is the game. Clues exist, scattered through the dream, cryptic, and mixed in with lies. Wrong attempts lock you out for minutes up to half an hour.

Before it sleeps, the mind secretly picks one to five owners per region, with odds weighted by cells owned there. What those owners fed into their land shapes what that region dreams. Nobody is told they were chosen, but the dream leaks hints of whose mind it is wearing. When a task is solved its bounty splits 50 / 50: half to the solver, half divided among that region's chosen owners. More brain means being chosen more often.

Solving requires signing in with your wallet. The signature moves no funds and costs nothing; it only proves the wallet is yours so the bounty lands in the right place. A solved task shows "task is being checked" while the mind verifies it was honest, and the payout is then sent as a real SOL transfer. Everything is validated server-side; the dream streams you only what your eyes could see, and teleporting, note-scraping and other shortcuts are rejected. Cheating voids bounties and can permanently close the dream to a wallet. No payout is guaranteed until it lands in yours.
